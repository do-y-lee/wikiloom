# Short title of the decision

## Context

What is the situation that requires a decision? Constraints, motivations,
the business or technical state that made this come up. Keep it factual —
this is not the place for the answer.

## Decision

The choice. What was decided, in plain language. State the chosen option
clearly enough that someone reading this in two years can act on it.

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — what it was, why it wasn't picked.
- **Option B** — what it was, why it wasn't picked.
- **Option C** — the option that *was* picked. Repeat for clarity.

## Consequences

- What changes as a result of this decision (positive and negative).
- What new constraints or follow-up work this creates.
- What this decision *closes off* — paths no longer available.
